## Loan Amount Distribution

### Observation
- The distribution of loan amounts is positively skewed.
- Most loans fall between ₹10,000 and ₹20,000.
- Very large loans are relatively uncommon.

### Business Insight
The bank primarily serves customers requesting small to medium-sized loans, indicating that its portfolio is concentrated in lower-risk lending segments.

### Recommendation
The bank can continue strengthening products targeted at small-loan customers while evaluating opportunities for profitable high-value lending.
